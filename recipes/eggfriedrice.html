Egg Fried Rice

Rice and egg fried together with light seasoning and fresh veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups white or brown rice
  • 1 diced green onion
  • 1 diced carrot
  • 1 or 2 fried eggs
  • 1-2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: Red Chili flakes
    •

    Steps

    1. Cook rice and set aside to dry and cool off.
    2. Dice green onion and set aside.
    3. Dice carrot and set aside.
    4. Scramble one or two eggs in a small pan and set aside.
    5. On high, heat a large wok or frying pan with cooking oil.
    6. Once hot, toss in carrots. Follow with pre-cooked rice. Stir and cook for 3 minutes
    7. Add pre-cooked eggs and stir.
    8. Add green onion, salt, and red chili flakes to taste.
    9. Serve!
