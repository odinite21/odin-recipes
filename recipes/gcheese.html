Grilled Cheese

Grilled sourdough bread with melted cheddar cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 slices of cheddar cheese or favorite cheese
  • Sliced sourdough bread
  • Butter
  • Optional: Creamy tomato soup
    •

    Steps

    1. Heat frying pan or skillet to medium/medium-high heat
    2. While pan is heating, butter one side of each bread slide
    3. Set buttered side of bread on hot pan. Add cheese and let sit until crispy/golden brown
    4. Add top slice of bread and repeat previous step, checking to see that cheese is melting evenly and bread is toasting to a nice golden brown
    5. Serve with hot creamy tomato soup or sauce of preference
